About Us

Dara Christian High School is a Christian-founded private secondary school in Lira, Uganda, located 6km along the Lira–Apala road at the foot of Ngetta hill.

Founded in 2003 by David G. Opiokello and Racheal Opiokello, it has consistently ranked among the top schools in Lira.

Core Values

  • Christ-like lifestyle
  • Accountability & Transparency
  • Team work
  • Professionalism, ethics & integrity
  • Leadership by example
  • Continuous learning & improvement
  • Disciplined conduct & Hardwork

Vision

“Be a leading and preferred provider of post primary school educational services in the East African sub-region.”

Mission

“To provide training opportunities to able and willing students that result into creativity and consistently excellent performance in a conducive environment in which Jesus is Lord.”

History

From humble beginnings in 2003, Dara Christian High School has grown into a beacon of excellence—nurturing disciplined, Christ-centered leaders and achieving top rankings in Lira.

Academics

We offer both O’level and A’level, aligned to the current Ministry of Education & Sports and NCDC curriculum.

O’level (S1 – S4)

Students undertake optional subjects such as ICT, Kiswahili, Physical Education, Entrepreneurship Education, Christian Religious Education, and Agriculture.

A’level (S5 – S6)

We offer a variety of subject combinations for both Arts and Sciences, supporting diverse career paths and university readiness. Best learning and research activities

Holistic Development

Discipline, leadership by example, and co-curricular engagements—including sports like ball games, acrobatics and aerobics and debate—build character and excellence.
